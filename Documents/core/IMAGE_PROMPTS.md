# AI Image Prompt Library

This document serves as the "Source of Truth" for maintaining a consistent visual identity across Muffin Pan Recipes. All images should follow the "Editorial Cookbook" aesthetic.

## üé® The "Editorial" Visual Language
- **Backgrounds:** Clean, high-key, white marble or light wood.
- **Lighting:** Natural side-lit daylight, high-key, soft shadows.
- **Composition:** Macro focus on texture, shallow depth of field (85mm, f/2.8).
- **Vibe:** Rustic but clean, minimalist, professional food styling.
