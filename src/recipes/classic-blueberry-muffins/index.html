Classic Blueberry Muffin Tops

Jump to Recipe

Why Muffin Pans?

The gold standard: bursting with berries and a perfectly domed top.

Prep 15 mins
Cook 20 mins
Yield 12 standard muffins

Ingredients

  • 250g Flour
  • 150g Sugar
  • 120ml Milk
  • 150g Blueberries

Instructions

  1. 01Preheat oven to 400°F.
  2. 02Mix dry ingredients.
  3. 03Whisk wet ingredients.
  4. 04Fold in blueberries.
  5. 05Bake for 20 mins.
